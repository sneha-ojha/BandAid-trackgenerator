# How to Contribute

I am excited you want to help! Here's how to get started:

1.  **Find an issue:** Look for issues tagged `help wanted` or `good first issue` on our [Issues Page](https://github.com/sneha-ojha/BandAid-trackgenerator/issues).
2.  **Fork & Branch:** Fork the repository and create a new branch for your work.
3.  **Make Your Change:** Write your code!
4.  **Submit a Pull Request:** Push your branch and submit a Pull Request. Please link to the issue you are fixing in your PR description.

Thank you for contributing!
